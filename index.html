Avoid Stepping on Toes

2021 July 17

When joining a new team, first find out where all the toes are at, then avoid stepping on them. Here's a possible script to use with your new teammates:

Possible script

"Hey, may I speak candidly? Because of [ reasons ] I won't be at this team, long-term. Until then I will do my best in [ description of job ]. At the same time, I'd like to learn as much as I can about our company.

"In order to learn about [ company ] I'd like to explore all the git repositories, and read all the documentation that I can. In the process of doing this, I may offend someone by, for example, [ three examples of things that may cause offense ] (some examples). Please tell me about other situations that would be frowned upon."

Footnotes

some examples At my current employer the example offenses could be, 1) Storing the personally identifiable information of customers on my work laptop, 2) Yelling at our shared manager, and 3) Accidentally causing the internal app we work on to go down in PROD.
